📊 Visualizing the History of Nobel Prize Winners

The Nobel Prize has recognized groundbreaking achievements since 1901 across Chemistry, Literature, Physics, Physiology or Medicine, Economics, and Peace. Beyond prestige and prize money, each laureate receives a gold medal honoring Alfred Nobel, the founder of the prize.

For this project, I analyzed the official dataset provided by the Nobel Foundation (1901–2023), sourced from the Nobel Prize API. Using data cleaning, aggregation, and visualization techniques, I explored historical trends and patterns in the data.

🔎 Key Insights Explored

Most commonly awarded gender and birth country
Decade with the highest proportion of US-born laureates
Decade and category with the highest share of female winners
First woman to win a Nobel Prize and her category
Individuals and organizations that have won multiple prizes

This project highlights my ability to transform raw historical data into meaningful insights through exploratory data analysis and compelling visual storytelling.
